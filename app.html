The Hundo App

How it works

Type any two numbers from 0 to 100 in the fields to the right, ensuring that the number you place in the second field is larger than the one you placed in the first. Once both fields are filled in with the numbers of your choice, click the yellow button.

The JavaScript code that powers Hundo will generate a list of numbers that will begin with the first number you chose and end with the second. All of the digits between the two numbers you provided will be displayed in the Results field, with even numbers represented in bold font.
